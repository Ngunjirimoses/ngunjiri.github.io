Peugeot 308

Peugeot 308 is a very simple French automobile that drives seamlessly with the 1.2L turbocharged petrol engine producing 128hp which is quite decent for this car. It is very spacious being the station wagon variant which makes it a good fit for family use and errands. It is also very comfortable and adventurous having a moonroof that extends to the rear passengers.
